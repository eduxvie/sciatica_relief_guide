8 Gentle Sciatica Relief Exercises

Follow along with these proven moves to ease sciatica pain naturally.
No equipment needed — just a few minutes a day.

1. Pelvic Tilt

Beginner • 10–15 reps
Lie on back → gently flatten lower back → release.

2. Knee to Chest

20–30s each side
Gentle lumbar decompression + glute stretch.

3. Supine Figure-4

30–60s each side
Targets the piriformis muscle directly.

4. Child’s Pose

30–90s
Spinal traction and deep relaxation.

5. Cat-Cow

8–10 breaths
Improves spinal mobility.

6. Glute Bridge

10–15 reps
Strengthens glutes & core.

7. Seated Spinal Twist

20–30s each side
Releases lumbar tension.

8. Standing Hamstring Stretch

20–30s each leg
Lengthens tight hamstrings.
