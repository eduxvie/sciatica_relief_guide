8 Gentle Sciatica Relief Exercises

Follow along with these proven moves to ease sciatica pain naturally.
No equipment needed — just a few minutes a day.

Important Medical Disclaimer

This content is for informational purposes only and is not medical advice.

Consult your doctor or physical therapist before starting any exercise program, especially if you have sciatica, back pain, or any health condition.

Stop immediately if you feel any pain or discomfort.

By continuing you agree to the disclaimer above.

1. Pelvic Tilt

Beginner • 10–15 reps
Lie on back → gently flatten lower back → release.

2. Knee to Chest

20–30s each side
Gentle lumbar decompression + glute stretch.

3. Supine Figure-4

30–60s each side
Targets the piriformis muscle directly.

4. Child’s Pose

30–90s
Spinal traction and deep relaxation.

5. Cat-Cow

8–10 breaths
Improves spinal mobility.

6. Glute Bridge

10–15 reps
Strengthens glutes & core.

7. Seated Spinal Twist

20–30s each side
Releases lumbar tension.

8. Standing Hamstring Stretch

20–30s each leg
Lengthens tight hamstrings.
